Harmonizer

Welcome to the best music harmonizer (by 4 very sleep-deprived first years)! This application allows you to create your own music by selecting the notes in the table below and if you haven't a clue about the first thing to do with music (like our very confused front-end dev), the notes for each piano key are shown when you hover over a key. Once you've made what I'm sure is a beautiful melody (or heinous war crime which is just a spam of notes), you can play it by clicking SPACEBAR. To change the tempo (speed) of your creation, you can edit the beats per minute. Finally to improve on your piece, you have the option to press the button 'Add chords' below which, based on your melody, selects the most appropriate chords to compliment your piece.
